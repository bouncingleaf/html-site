About... Leaf?

Years ago, a friend called me by my username, which was jroy. I said: "Hey, if you're not going to call me by my name, at least come up with a new name for me."

A few days later, said friend gave me a new name: Bouncing Leaf. "Leaf," for short. I used that online, it stuck.
